Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about custom sizing, ordering, and delivery.

How is pricing calculated?

Most zebra blinds are custom. Your estimate is calculated using your saved measurements (width and height) and the base rate shown on the product card.

Do I need to measure before ordering?

Yes. Add an item to cart, then enter your window measurements in Quick View. The cart and checkout will use those saved sizes.

Can I change measurements later?

Yes. Open the product Quick View again, enter the new size, then add to cart. The latest saved size will be used.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact page or the contact section on the homepage.
